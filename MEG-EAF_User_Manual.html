1. Overview

The MyEden Employment Application Form (MEG-EAF) is a single-page web tool used by job applicants to submit their employment application, and by HR/admin staff to review applications, record hiring decisions, and automatically generate the correct Appointment Letter (Teacher or Management Staff) once a candidate is approved.

The form works entirely in your web browser — no installation is required. It auto-saves your progress as you type, and a final submission is saved either as a downloadable JSON draft file or directly to the Company's Google Drive folder.

This tool runs as a single web page. You do not need an account to fill in an application — only HR/admin staff need a password to unlock the restricted sections.

2. Getting Started

Open the form's web link in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari are recommended). The form is divided into several pages, separated by dashed lines — scroll down to move between them. A short reference code and today's date appear automatically at the top.

Fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are mandatory. If you try to save a draft or print without completing them, a red error box will appear at the top of the page listing exactly what is missing, and the relevant fields will be highlighted in red.

3. Filling in the Application (Applicant Guide)

3.1 Personal Information

Enter your full name in English (required) and Chinese (optional, if applicable). For your I/C number, type only the digits — the three boxes (YYMMDD / XX / XXXX) will auto-format as you type. If you are a foreign applicant without a Malaysian I/C, leave the I/C field blank and fill in your Passport No. instead.

If you enter your I/C number, your Date of Birth is automatically extracted and filled in for you (you can still edit it). If you only have a Passport No., you must manually enter your Date of Birth.

Complete your Gender, Marital Status, Race, and Religion using the radio buttons. If your situation isn't listed, select "Others" and type your answer in the box that appears next to it.

Under "Residential Address," fill in Address Line 1 (required), Address Line 2 and 3 (optional, for longer addresses), and your Postcode & Town and State (both required). These fields are used later to print your address correctly on official documents such as the Appointment Letter, so please write them clearly.

3.2 Uploading Your Photo

Click the passport-photo box at the top-right of the form. You can choose "Camera" to take a photo directly (on a phone/tablet) or "Gallery" to upload an existing image. Click "Clear" to remove the photo and try again.

3.3 Family & Academic Information

Fill in the Family Information table with details of your immediate family members. If a column doesn't apply (e.g. you have no dependents), simply type "N/A". Click "+ Add family member" to add more rows if needed.

Do the same for the Academic Achievements table, listing your education history from the most basic to the highest qualification obtained. Mark "Y" or "N" under "Graduated" for each entry.

3.4 Job Requirements

Tick the position(s) you are applying for under "Intended Post." You may select more than one. If your desired role isn't listed, tick "Others" and describe it in the box provided.

Select your Desired Working Hour type (Full Time, Part Time, Internship, or Temporary), then fill in your preferred Working Hours (From/To) and Expected Salary. You may also indicate when you are available to start.

Tick the languages you can speak and write. If a language isn't listed, type it in the "Others" box.

3.5 Special Skills & Work Experience

Use the free-text box to describe any special skills relevant to the position. Under Work Experience, click "+ Add more experience" for each previous job you'd like to list, filling in the employer, job title, dates, salary, duties, and reason for leaving.

3.6 Other Details

Answer all four Yes/No questions honestly (bankruptcy, criminal conviction, serious illness, physical handicap). If you answer "Yes" to any of them, provide details in the box below. Also indicate if you have any relatives currently employed at MyEden Group, and how you found out about this vacancy.

3.7 Declaration & Signature

Read the Declaration box carefully, then tick the checkbox confirming you understand and agree to it. Fill in your name and the date, then sign using your mouse, finger, or stylus directly on the signature box. Click "Clear Signature" if you need to redo it.

3.8 Identification Document Photos

Upload clear photos of the front and back of your I/C. If you are using a passport instead, tick "Using Passport instead of I/C" first — the labels will change to "Passport — Photo Page" (required) and "Passport — Other Page" (optional).

3.9 Saving Your Draft

Once all required fields are completed, click 💾 Save Draft at the bottom of the form. This downloads a .json file containing all your answers (including photos and signature) — please send this file to HR as instructed, or keep it so you can continue later.

The "Load Draft," "Save to Drive," and "Print / Save PDF" buttons are reserved for HR/admin use and will not appear until an admin logs in (see Section 4).

4. Admin / HR Guide

4.1 Logging in as Admin

Click the 🔒 Admin button at the bottom of the form and enter the admin password provided to you by management. Once logged in, a green ✅ Admin Mode badge appears, the "For Office Use Only" section unlocks, and several extra buttons appear in the action bar.

4.2 Office Use Section

Fill in who interviewed the candidate, the hiring Decision (Approved / Disapproved / KIV / Others), the Final Approved Salary, the confirmed Working Hours, Start Date, Department, and which documents were collected. Add any internal comments in the Comments box.

4.3 Appointment Letter Details

This sub-section, found just below "Comments," controls how the Appointment Letter is generated.

Approved Job Title & Detected Letter Type

Type the candidate's final, approved job title here. As soon as you open Admin Mode, this field defaults to whatever the applicant ticked under "Intended Post" — you should review and correct it if needed. The system automatically detects which letter template to use based on keywords found in this title:

If the Approved Job Title contains...Letter generated
"Teacher", "Childcare", "Mentor", "Infant", or "Playgroup" (any case)Teacher Appointment Letter
Anything else (e.g. "Accounts Executive", "Office Admin")Management Staff Appointment Letter

The result is shown live next to the field as "Detected Letter Type."

Override Detected Letter Type

If the automatic detection is wrong for a particular case, tick "Override detected letter type" — a dropdown will appear letting you manually force the letter to be either Teacher or Management Staff, regardless of what the job title says.

Reporting Officer

Enter the name and job title of the person the new hire will report to. This appears in Clause 2 ("Reporting") of the Appointment Letter.

Salary in Wording

This field auto-fills in words (e.g. "Ringgit Malaysia Two Thousand Eight Hundred Only") as soon as you type a number into "Final Approved Salary." You can edit it manually afterwards if you need to correct the wording — once you do, it will stop auto-updating even if you change the salary again.

Appendix 1 — Scope of Duties (Management roles only)

This text box only appears when the Detected Letter Type is "Management Staff." Type or paste the job-specific scope of duties for this role — this becomes Appendix 1 of the printed letter. (Teacher roles use a fixed, pre-written Appendix 1 & 2 and do not need this field.)

4.4 Generating & Printing the Appointment Letter

Scroll down to the green "Appointment Letter (Auto-Generated Preview)" section near the bottom of the form. This shows a live preview of the full letter (cover page, terms & conditions, acceptance page, and appendix) using everything you've filled in so far. It updates automatically as you type.

When ready, click 📋 Print Appointment Letter. The system will check that all required fields (Approved Job Title, Reporting Officer, Salary in Wording, full address, and the Appendix if applicable) are filled in — if anything is missing, an error list appears and nothing will print. Once everything is complete, your browser's print dialog opens showing only the letter (with the Company letterhead on every page), ready to print or "Save as PDF."

Only the letter is printed in this mode — the rest of the application form is automatically hidden so you don't waste paper or confuse the candidate.

4.5 Printing / Saving the Full Application as PDF

Click 🖶 Print / Save PDF to print or save the entire completed application form (not just the letter). The same validation rules apply, plus a few extra Office Use fields become mandatory at this stage (Decision, Final Salary, Working Hours, Start Date, Department).

4.6 Saving to Google Drive

Click ☁ Save to Drive. The Client ID and Folder ID fields are pre-filled — you normally don't need to change them. Choose whether to save the Draft JSON, a PDF, or both, then click "Save to Drive." The first time you do this, Google will ask you to log in and grant permission.

4.7 Loading a Draft

Click 📂 Load Draft to either browse and load a previously saved draft from the Company's Google Drive folder, or choose a .json file from your own device. All fields, photos, and the signature will be restored exactly as they were saved.

4.8 Locking Admin Mode

When you're done, click the 🔒 Lock button inside the green Admin Mode badge. This re-locks the Office Use section and hides all admin-only buttons and the Appointment Letter preview, returning the form to its normal applicant view.

1. 概述

MyEden 求职申请表（MEG-EAF）是一个单页网页工具，供求职者提交求职申请，同时供人事/管理员审核申请、记录录用决定，并在候选人获批后自动生成相应的聘用信（教师类 或 管理/行政类）。

本工具完全在浏览器中运行，无需安装任何软件。系统会在您填写时自动保存进度，最终提交可下载为 JSON 草稿文件，或直接保存到公司的 Google Drive 文件夹。

本工具为单一网页。填写申请表无需账号登录——只有人事/管理员需要密码才能解锁受限区域。

2. 开始使用

请使用现代浏览器（建议使用 Chrome、Edge 或 Safari）打开表单链接。表单分为多个页面，以虚线分隔——向下滚动即可切换页面。页面顶部会自动显示参考编号与当天日期。

标有红色星号（*）的字段为必填项。若在未填妥的情况下尝试保存草稿或打印，页面顶部会出现红色错误提示框，列出缺漏的项目，相关字段也会以红色标示。

3. 填写申请表（申请人指南）

3.1 个人资料

请填写英文全名（必填）及中文姓名（如适用，选填）。身份证号码只需输入数字，三个输入框（出生年月日 / 出生地代码 / 序号）会自动格式化。若您是没有马来西亚身份证的外籍申请人，请将身份证号码留空，改填护照号码。

若您填写了身份证号码，系统会自动提取并填入您的出生日期（仍可手动修改）。若只填写护照号码，则必须手动输入出生日期。

请使用单选按钮填写性别、婚姻状况、种族与宗教。若选项中没有符合您情况的选择，请选择"其他"并在旁边的输入框中填写说明。

在"居住地址"部分，请填写地址第一行（必填）、地址第二、第三行（选填，供较长地址使用）、以及邮政编码与城镇、州属（皆为必填）。这些资料将用于日后在正式文件（如聘用信）上准确打印您的地址，请清楚填写。

3.2 上传照片

点击表单右上方的护照照片框。您可以选择"Camera"（相机）直接拍照（适用于手机/平板），或选择"Gallery"（图库）上传现有图片。点击"Clear"（清除）可移除照片并重新上传。

3.3 家庭与学历资料

请在家庭资料表中填写直系亲属的详情。若某栏不适用（例如无须供养的家属），请填写"N/A"。如需更多行，点击"+ Add family member"（新增家庭成员）。

学历资料表同理，请由最基本到最高学历依序列出。每一项学历请在"Graduated"（是否毕业）栏中标示"Y"或"N"。

3.4 职位需求

请在"Intended Post"（应征职位）勾选您申请的职位，可勾选多项。若您想申请的职位未列出，请勾选"Others"（其他）并在提供的输入框中说明。

请选择期望的工作性质（全职、兼职、实习或临时），并填写期望的工作时间（起/止）与期望薪资。您也可注明可上班的日期。

请勾选您能使用的口语与书写语言。若语言未列出，请在"Others"输入框中填写。

3.5 特殊技能与工作经验

请在文字框中描述与该职位相关的特殊技能。在工作经验部分，每填写一份过往工作经历，请点击一次"+ Add more experience"（新增工作经验），填写雇主、职位、起止日期、薪资、工作内容及离职原因。

3.6 其他详情

请如实回答四道是非题（破产记录、刑事记录、严重疾病、身体残疾）。若任一题回答"Yes"，请在下方文字框说明详情。同时请注明是否有亲属目前在 MyEden Group 任职，以及您是从何处得知此职位空缺。

3.7 声明与签名

请仔细阅读声明内容，然后勾选确认您已阅读并同意该声明。填写您的姓名及日期，再使用鼠标、手指或触控笔在签名框中签名。如需重签，点击"Clear Signature"（清除签名）。

3.8 身份证件照片

请上传身份证正反面的清晰照片。若改用护照，请先勾选"Using Passport instead of I/C"（改用护照），标签将自动改为"护照——照片页"（必填）及"护照——其他页"（选填）。

3.9 保存草稿

所有必填项完成后，点击表单底部的💾 Save Draft（保存草稿）。系统将下载一份包含您所有填写内容（含照片与签名）的 .json 文件——请依指示将此文件发送给人事部，或自行保留以便日后继续填写。

"Load Draft"（载入草稿）、"Save to Drive"（保存至 Drive）及"Print / Save PDF"（打印/保存 PDF）按钮仅供人事/管理员使用，登录管理员模式后才会显示（详见第 4 章）。

4. 管理员/人事指南

4.1 登录管理员模式

点击表单底部的🔒 Admin按钮，输入管理层提供给您的管理员密码。登录成功后，将出现绿色的✅ Admin Mode标志，"For Office Use Only"（办公室专用）区域将解锁，操作栏中也会出现额外按钮。

4.2 办公室专用区

请填写面试官、录用决定（Approved 批准 / Disapproved 拒绝 / KIV 保留 / Others 其他）、最终核准薪资、确认的工作时间、入职日期、部门，以及已收取的文件。如有内部备注，可填写在 Comments（备注）框中。

4.3 聘用信详情

此小节位于"Comments"（备注）下方，用于控制聘用信的自动生成方式。

核准职位名称 与 检测到的信函类型

请在此填写候选人最终核准的职位名称。进入管理员模式后，此字段会自动预填申请人在"Intended Post"中勾选的职位——请检查并在需要时修正。系统会根据此职位名称中的关键字自动判断应使用哪一份聘用信模板：

若核准职位名称中包含...将生成的聘用信
"Teacher"（教师）、"Childcare"（幼教/托管）、"Mentor"（导师）、"Infant"（婴幼儿）或"Playgroup"（游戏班）（不分大小写）教师类聘用信
其他任何名称（例如"Accounts Executive"会计执行员、"Office Admin"行政人员）管理/行政类聘用信

判断结果会即时显示在该字段旁的"Detected Letter Type"（检测到的信函类型）处。

手动覆盖检测结果

若系统自动判断的类型在某个案例中并不正确，请勾选"Override detected letter type"（覆盖检测结果）——届时会出现下拉选单，让您手动指定使用教师类或管理/行政类聘用信，不受职位名称影响。

直属上司

请填写新员工将汇报对象的姓名与职位。此资料将出现在聘用信第 2 条款（"Reporting" 汇报关系）中。

薪资文字（大写）

只要您在"Final Approved Salary"（最终核准薪资）中输入数字，此字段便会自动以文字形式填入（例如："Ringgit Malaysia Two Thousand Eight Hundred Only"）。如需修改文字内容，可直接手动编辑——编辑后，即使再次更改薪资数字，此字段也不会再自动更新。

附录一 — 职务范围（仅限管理/行政类职位）

此文字框仅在检测到的信函类型为"管理/行政类"时才会显示。请在此输入或粘贴该职位的具体职务范围——此内容将成为打印版聘用信的附录一。（教师类职位使用固定预设的附录一及附录二，无需填写此字段。）

4.4 生成与打印聘用信

向下滚动至表单底部绿色的"Appointment Letter (Auto-Generated Preview)"（聘用信自动生成预览）区域。此处会根据您目前已填写的所有资料，即时显示完整信函预览（封面页、聘用条款、接受聘约页及附录），并随您输入内容自动更新。

准备就绪后，点击📋 Print Appointment Letter（打印聘用信）。系统将检查所有必填项目（核准职位名称、直属上司、薪资文字、完整地址，以及管理/行政类职位所需的附录内容）是否齐全——若有缺漏，将显示错误清单，且不会打印。所有项目齐全后，浏览器将打开打印对话框，仅显示聘用信内容（每页均附公司信头），即可打印或"另存为 PDF"。

此模式下只会打印聘用信本身——申请表的其余部分会自动隐藏，避免浪费纸张或造成候选人混淆。

4.5 打印/保存完整申请表为 PDF

点击🖶 Print / Save PDF（打印/保存 PDF）可打印或保存整份已完成的申请表（非仅聘用信）。此操作适用相同的验证规则，且此阶段会有额外几项"Office Use"字段变为必填（录用决定、最终薪资、工作时间、入职日期、部门）。

4.6 保存至 Google Drive

点击☁ Save to Drive（保存至 Drive）。Client ID 与 Folder ID 字段已预先填妥，通常无需更改。请选择要保存草稿 JSON、PDF，或两者皆保存，然后点击"Save to Drive"。首次操作时，Google 会要求您登录并授权。

4.7 载入草稿

点击📂 Load Draft（载入草稿），可浏览并载入公司 Google Drive 文件夹中先前保存的草稿，或从您自己的设备选择 .json 文件。所有字段、照片及签名都会恢复至保存时的状态。

4.8 锁定管理员模式

完成操作后，请点击绿色 Admin Mode 标志内的🔒 Lock（锁定）按钮。此操作将重新锁定"Office Use"区域，并隐藏所有管理员专用按钮及聘用信预览，使表单恢复为一般申请人视图。
